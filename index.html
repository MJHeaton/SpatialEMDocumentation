Spatial EM

Documentation site for the Spatial EM framework: a latent-Gaussian EM approach that combines flexible machine-learning mean models with spatial dependence (via Vecchia approximations and whitening).

Quick mental map

  • Fit a model with fit_spatial_em() (choose family and ml_model).
  • Predict with predict() on "response", "link", or "latent_w" scales.
  • Tune spatial + ML hyperparameters with tune_spatial_em(), then parse the best config with extract_best_config().
  • Under the hood: Vecchia structure, whitening, and reconstruction utilities.
